## Cross-cutting decisions baked in

- **Compound surge**: zone × (1 + 0.5 × eventBoost) × night with a hard cap at **3.5×**. Tests assert this bound at the worst-case spot (Allegiant during a Raiders night game).
- **Client-side scoring is gameable.** Every services file's header makes this explicit. The future server-side matching engine must `implements MatchingEngine` (defined in `assistant-matching.ts`) and recompute from authoritative inputs before any payout-affecting decision.
- **Tourist Mode is opt-in.** `isLikelyTourist` only emits a 0..1 score; the UI never auto-enables.
- **Safety timer is foreground-only.** Documented in `mobile-platform-risks.md` §0; needs `expo-task-manager` background fetch as follow-up.
- **Route-sharing is local UI only.** The toggle does not transmit anything yet; real route-share needs a backend channel + on-chain opt-in PDA flag.
- **Polygons are approximate.** Drawn from memory; refine with city GIS before any pricing claim that depends on zone boundaries.
